📖 Quick guide — value, edge & Kelly

Value bet — The book’s price suggests a lower chance than our model. If we think an outcome is more likely than the odds imply, there may be a positive edge.

Edge % — Model chance minus the book’s implied chance (from decimal odds). Example: we say 55%, book implies 45% → +10% edge. We only flag value when edge clears a safety margin.

ROI % — Expected long-run return if our probabilities are right — a paper advantage, not a guarantee.

Kelly % — A staking guide, not a prediction. Suggests a sensible fraction of bankroll for this edge (we use fractional Kelly, capped). Labels:

  • Strong — Clear edge; larger (still capped) suggested stake.
  • Moderate — Usable edge; moderate suggested stake.
  • Cautious — Thin edge; small stake only.
  • Skip — No positive edge on this line.
